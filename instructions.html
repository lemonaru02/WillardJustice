Instruction Set for New Instructors

In the first semester of appointment as a graduate part-time instructor (GPTI), one of the most daunting tasks is to assess student work. There are challenges with understanding the university and programmatic grading policies, coming to terms with assigning value to student work, formulating helpful feedback for students, and navigating the grading tools in the LMS. This instruction set attempts to address each of those challenges to provide a solid foundation for incoming GPTIs, with the understanding that context-specific issues may be better instructed on through the FYW scaffolded support system. A member of the FYW administration would give the presentation during the typical one-week pre-semester orientation, and the handout and video guide would be made available to all FYW GPTIs on the SharePoint for future reference. Presentation guidance for effective instruction is included in the PowerPoint notes under each slide.

The instruction set is meant to be taught to incoming GPTIs in the Texas Tech first-year writing (FYW) program. The instruction set viability report is for the hypothetical client of the FYW administration team.

This project originated as an assignment for the course Instructional Design for Technical Communicators.
